The Shop

Where Iron
Meets Craft

IRON & FADE lives in the historic Old Iron Works building at 112 W Main St — a structure that has stood since the 1920s. Original brick, exposed timber beams, and antique cast-iron fixtures. The atmosphere doesn't pretend to be anything it isn't.

We built this shop for the working man who values his time and respects a trade done right. You'll smell sandalwood and bay rum, hear delta blues on the record player, and leave looking like your best self. Cell phones go away when you sit down.
